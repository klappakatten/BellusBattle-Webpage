Who are we?

We are a group of Highly dedicated and motivated gamedevelopers based in Sweden. We created this project as part of a course at Stockholm University where we recieved excellent response. During development of Bellus Battle we conducted 6 official playtests where 4 of them where open to the public. Our latest 2 tests where conducted at the Tekniska museet where our accessablity features really helped the kids have a great time. Our vision is to develop and release the full game for everyone to enjoy!
