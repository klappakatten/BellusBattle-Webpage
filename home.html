What is Bellus Battle?

Bellus Battle is a fast-paced fighting party game where up to 4 players battle it out to be the last one standing. Players can pick up various unqiue weapons scattered around the arena to gain an advantage and destroy their enemy.

Bellus Battle have a good collection of crazy weapons and levels to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting. The design focus for us is on creating fun and entertaining weapons that are both enjoyable to use and face. The local multiplayer allows up to four players to play simultaneously which adds alot of chaos and fun. The game is round-based and played with how many number of rounds you'd like.

  • Revolver
  • Grenade Launcher
  • Railgun

The backstory involves animals fighting each other to the death for the entertainment of humans who bet on the winner. Players can choose from different animals ranging from lost pets to wildlife savages.

Playable Characters

Panda
The unofficial main character of Bellus arena, he might not look dangerous but he has won the crown several times already. The panda is always looking for a good fight.

Pig
This little piggy grew up in a rough neighborhood where you had to be really tough to survive. Unfortunately the little piggy tends to be very scared. Got caught stealing and was therefore sentenced to the arena

Monkey
This little monkey is always angry for some reason, why? nobody knows and no one person is brave enough to ask him... some think the monkey attends the arena for fun..?!

Cat
Indoor cat that went on a adventure to find his owners gone when he came back, he now has to do whatever it takes to survive

The game world has a toonshader that allows for fun and varied themes for the levels. The levels come in small, medium, and large sizes, and there are various weapon spawns that are based on the level, with some randomization.
